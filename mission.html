MISSION



1. Craft and manage an induction process for all new joiners so that they are well integrated into the organization and into the team.


2. Prepare, update, and recommend HR operational guidelines to run the weekly/monthly and yearly activities.


3. Conduct exit interviews, collect feedback and proactively suggest ways to arrest undesired experiences.


4. Ensure HR-related documentation is organized and accurate, backed up, and fully compliant in HRIS and other related tools.


5. Conduct monthly townhall, weekly employee engagement activities and other forums/events Ensures organization announcements and communication reaches every employee.


6. Handle grievances, counsel employees, and take disciplinary actions as and when required Handling statutory compliance - PF, ESI, etc


7. Manage the payroll and other employee benefits administration.


8. Develop and manage channels to gather employee feedback from time to time


9. Develop, drive and track the HR programs until closure.
